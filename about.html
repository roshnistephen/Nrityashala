Smt. Preeti Mohapatra

Odissi Exponent & Guru

Biography

A Life Devoted to Dance

✦

"An artist whose journey reflects an unwavering dedication to tradition, discipline, and creative excellence."

Mrs. Preeti Mohapatra, an accomplished Odissi exponent, stands among the illustrious disciples of the legendary gurus Padma Vibhushan Guru Sri Kelucharan Mohapatra and Guru Sri Naba Kishore Mishra. A recipient of the National Scholarship for both Odissi and Chhau, she embodies the true essence of India's classical dance heritage.

National Scholarship – Odissi Dance
National Scholarship – Chhau Dance
Sangit Prabhakar, Prayag Sangit Samiti
Graded Artist, Doordarshan Bhubaneswar
40+ years of artistic journey
25+ years as dedicated Odissi Guru

Over a 40-year artistic journey, including 25 years as a dedicated Odissi guru, she has carved a niche as both a performer of exceptional grace and a teacher of rare depth. A Sangit Prabhakar from Prayag Sangit Samiti Prayagraj, Mrs. Mohapatra has presented her art at prestigious cultural platforms across India.

Prestigious Performances & Festivals

  • Konark Festival
  • Puri Beach Festival
  • India International Trade Fair, Pragati Maidan
  • International Odissi Dance Festival, Bhubaneswar
  • Naba Kruti, Bhubaneswar
  • Natyanjali Festival, Chidambaram

Smt. Preeti Mohapatra is also a graded artist of Doordarshan, Bhubaneswar, a distinction accorded to artists of exceptional calibre and contribution to classical arts.

For the past 15 years, she has been nurturing the next generation of Odissi dancers at Nrityashala, a renowned Hyderabad-based institution dedicated to authentic classical Odissi training. Her teaching philosophy is rooted in the Guru-Shishya parampara — the sacred tradition of knowledge passed from master to disciple.

In recognition of her creative contributions and her steadfast efforts to preserve and promote the Odissi tradition, Preeti Mohapatra was recently honoured with the prestigious "Natya Thoranam" award by the Amrita Cultural Trust, Hyderabad.
